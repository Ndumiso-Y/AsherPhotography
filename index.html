FAQ

Common Questions

How do I book a session?

Simply click the WhatsApp button or fill out our contact form. We'll discuss your vision, confirm availability, and secure your date with a 50% deposit.

What is your turnaround time?

Standard delivery is 5-7 days for edited photos. Rush delivery (48 hours) is available for an additional fee.

Do you travel outside Rustenburg?

Yes! I regularly shoot in surrounding areas including Brits, Sun City, and Pretoria. Travel fees apply for locations beyond 50km.

What happens if it rains?

We can reschedule at no extra cost, move to a covered location, or embrace the rain for unique dramatic shots. Your choice!

What should I wear?

I'll send you a detailed style guide after booking. Generally, solid colors work best, and we recommend bringing 2-3 outfit options.
